Company Introduction

Jiangsu Canasin Weaving Co., Ltd is a leading integrated linen supplier in Asia-pacific region that professionally focuses on inventing, producing, selling and servicing in the field of hotel textile.

Canasin has been emphasizing the importance of hotel linen since its establishment, and also devoted into creating elegant and comfortable environment for sleeping and dining together with hotels, which would bring unique and luxury experience for more business clients.

In nowadays, Canasin has presented high-quality suite bedding, bathroom towels, dining textiles and matched solutions for over 2,800 premium-end hotels, for the sake of which Canasin has spread its business scale from China to Europe, America, Oceania, Middle East and Southeast Asia, including over 40 countries and regions.At present, Canasin has built a producing area that occupies 133 thousand square meters and over 1,300 employees, it also established an integrated producing chain that including inventing, designing, material testing, spinning, bleaching and dyeing, sewing and sorting, product testing, logistics and other parts; all of these enable the corporation to lower clients’ risks and then realize the vertical integration of producing and operating.

As the main draft written company for China Hotel Linen National Standard (GB/T22800-2009 “Textiles for Stared Tourist Hotels”), Canasin also participated in drafting and deciding other standards such as GB/T22864-2009 “Towels” National Standard, FZ/T620011.2-2008 “Dining Textile” Industry Standard, for which China’s linen industry standards have finally been regulated in a positive way and pushed to improve the industry’s quality.

Canasin, based on its professional linen sorting solution, service and great and stable product quality, has become the recommended (recognized) linen supplier for international and China’s famous hotels like InterContinental, Starwood, Marriot, Hilton, Hyatt, Accor, Jinling and Kaiyuan.
